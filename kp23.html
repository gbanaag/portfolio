Figma Help Bar

"How do you provide support to first-time users navigating Figma?"

Focus

UX/UI

Timeframe

1 week, January 2023

Role

Product Designer, UX Researcher

Deliverable

To redesign a feature of a Kleiner Perkins company product. Hi, KP Fellows team!

I was tasked with redesigning a feature on Figma.

For the Kleiner Perkins Fellowship Design Challenge, I chose to redesign the help menu on the Figma web app from a UX/UI Design perspective.

I'm an avid Figma user, and I've always noticed that using the program is a bit confusing and difficult to navigate for first-time users and decided to explore how I could improve this aspect of Figma's user experience.

Figma was difficult to navigate for some first-time users.

While the app supported directly messaging other users, finding users through posts, and joining existing communities within the network, users were unable to create communities and groups of their own.
